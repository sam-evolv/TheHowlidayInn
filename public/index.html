About The Howliday Inn

Located in the heart of Kilgarvan, Curraghbinny, Carrigaline, Co. Cork, we provide premium care for your four-legged family members. Our passion is ensuring every dog feels loved, safe, and happy during their stay with us.

Licensed & Insured

Fully licensed and insured facility with experienced, trained staff who genuinely love dogs. We maintain the highest standards of care and safety.

Safe Environment

Secure, clean, and spacious facilities designed specifically for your dog's comfort and safety. All areas are regularly sanitized and maintained.

Personalized Care

Individual attention and care tailored to your dog's specific needs, personality, and preferences. Every dog gets the love they deserve.
